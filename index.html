ABOUT US

THE RESTAURANT

LooBapBap was born from a love for Korean comfort food and the desire to share it in a fresh, modern way. We blend traditional flavors with a fun, fusion twist — serving quick, satisfying meals that bring a taste of Seoul to Canada. Every dish is made with care, using quality ingredients and bold flavors that capture the warmth of homemade Korean cooking. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or your new favorite comfort meal, LooBapBap is all about tasty food, good vibes, and bringing people together through flavor.
